Process

Browse, generate, shop, and manage.

The Whimsleek experience guides users from inspiration to action in a clear sequence while keeping everything bright, playful, and privacy-aware.

Step 1

Discover the mood

Users begin with visuals, branding, and style cues that help them understand the Whimsleek aesthetic instantly.

Step 2

Generate a fit

They choose a vibe and occasion, then get a suggested outfit with matching pieces and finishing accessories.

Step 3

Review shop picks

From there, they can browse featured products, add pieces to their bag, and move into the storefront flow.

Step 4

Accept terms before access

Log-in and sign-up are gated by a Terms of Agreement step first, keeping the privacy flow visible and intentional.

Privacy First

Terms of Agreement
