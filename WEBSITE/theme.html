Theme

Glossy pink with a starry wink.

The theme leans into rosy gradients, romantic serif display type, playful handwritten script, and sparkly accents so the site feels fun, girly, and whimsical.

Typography

Fun fonts with fashion-editorial drama.

Playfair Display handles the big elegant headings, Pacifico adds that flirty handwritten energy, and Nunito keeps the rest clear and friendly.

Visual Mood

Soft glow, candy pink, bright cream.

The palette uses blush and bubblegum pinks with creamy panels so the content stays readable while still feeling sugary and cute.
