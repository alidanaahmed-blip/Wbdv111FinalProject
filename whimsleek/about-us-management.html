Guide the public brand story across the website.

About page focus

Explain the platform clearly.

The public story should make it obvious that Whimsleek is both a shopping site and an outfit generator, with separate user, admin, and super admin experiences.

Use enough context for first-time visitors to understand the brand.
Keep the color direction solid: cream, amber, orange, and matching pink.
Include real product or fashion imagery wherever a page needs trust.

Content rule

Use complete sections instead of placeholder copy.

About content should include what Whimsleek does, who it helps, how shopping and outfit generation connect, and why the account agreement exists.

Review queue

Keep updates practical and brand-aligned.

Before publishing changes, review navigation labels, image quality, color usage, agreement text, and whether the page has enough detail to feel legitimate.
