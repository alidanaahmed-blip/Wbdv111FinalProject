About Whimsleek

A fashion playground that still feels like a real store.

Whimsleek combines a playful outfit generator with a shoppable catalog, so visitors can move from inspiration to product choices without losing context.

Our idea

Make styling feel easier, brighter, and more personal.

The site is built for people who like expressive clothes but still want practical guidance. Each generated outfit includes a clear mood, a wearable reason, product photos, prices, and simple next steps.

Our color system stays warm and sunny with a matching pink accent for energy, calls to action, badges, and key agreement moments.

What shoppers can do

Generate, browse, save, and order.

Visitors can test style moods, add full looks to the cart, browse the expanded shop, contact support, and continue into a user dashboard for saved looks, profile details, and order tracking.

What the platform supports

Storefront, user area, admin area, and super admin tools.

Whimsleek includes management pages for products, orders, users, analytics, reports, system content, and about-page updates. That structure makes the project feel complete instead of like a single static landing page.

Brand promise

Playful design, clear navigation, and honest shopping context.

The site uses bright solid colors, readable sections, product imagery, useful descriptions, and role-based account paths so the experience feels polished and understandable.
