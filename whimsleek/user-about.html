About Us

Whimsleek exists to make fashion exploration feel easier and more fun.

The platform combines inspiration, shopping, saved-account behavior, and admin oversight so it feels closer to a real web product than a one-page concept.

For shoppers

Generate looks, browse products, and keep everything connected.

Users can move from public pages into account-only pages without losing the site’s style language or navigation logic.

For the platform

The back-office pages support the storefront behind the scenes.

Admins manage products, orders, users, analytics, reports, and content, while super admins control permissions and system-wide messaging.
